JAM-D-JAM is a new interactive call-in radio show format where you create new material entirely from live call-in samples from ATL rush-hour traffic.

The Jam-D-Jam team is currently developing this concept with leading Atlanta radio stations, the goal is to highlight talented Atlanta producers that can transform sounds contributed by trapped commuters into music that is looped back into the gridlock. We are seeking out talent that can utilize their sampling skills to create work that evolves over the course of a one-hour show.

Use the sample material available for download to quickly produce a 2-minute demo as if the calls were coming in live from traffic on 85.
